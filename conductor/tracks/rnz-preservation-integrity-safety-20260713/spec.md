# Track Specification: RNZ Preservation Integrity & Sensitive-Content Safety

## Requirements

- Represent one-to-many RNZ landing pages as parent records with independently checksummed child recordings.
- Add perceptual audio fingerprints and relationships for re-encodes, excerpts, rebroadcasts and repeated bulletins without deleting variants.
- Verify archive round trips, media decodability, duration, checksums and transcript offsets after publication.
- Add review flags for children, callers, victims, distressing material and potentially sensitive historical recordings without automated takedown.
- Preserve source metadata snapshots, feed relationships and provenance for every derivative.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Compound pages no longer become ambiguous retries or unrelated downloads.
- Duplicate relationships distinguish exact, perceptual, excerpt and rebroadcast matches.
- Sensitive-content signals create review tasks and never infer protected traits.
- Integrity audits can reconstruct and verify every published item.
